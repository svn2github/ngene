The more enemies you kill in a row, the higher the score multiplier.
All weapons but a Simple Rifle have limited ammo - remember of that.
The further you go, the more enemies you encounter.
Try to collect power-ups. You can find new weapons, extra points or health.
The most powerful weapon of the game is Lightning, you can't miss with that!
Avoid colliding with enemies, you can destroy them but you loose health too.
Shield power-up can be used as a weapon, you can hit enemies without penalty.
Enemies can use different weapons.
When close to death you will hear heart beating and screen will go black&white.
